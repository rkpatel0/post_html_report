Comparing PreRanks vs. League Settings

Below is a list of the optimal preranking for each league setting. Depending on what parameters are being compared you should be able see the difference in pre-rankings across each league. Evening minor changes like 2 WR + 3 RB vs. 3 WR + 2 RB can have major a signficant impact of prerankings.

One other thing that has a major impact on prerankings are player point totals. Whether that be players (1) from previous years, (2) simulated players - exponential vs. linear or (3) projected players. It is quiet important how to players drop off (slope) relaitve to one another. Many projects use a very basic linear approximation which are great for basic rankings but much of the signal gets lost in the noise.
